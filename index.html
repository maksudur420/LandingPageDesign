WHO WE ARE

AFTAB is an iconic poultry & process food brand that is loved and trusted by our consumers. It is owned by IG Foods Limited (IGFL). IGFL is a fully integrated poultry processing company engaged in the production, processing, marketing and distribution of fresh and frozen chicken and other prepared food items. IGFL follows high international operating standards, and is certified by ISO 22000:2018, Total Quality Management System and Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points (HACCP), UKAS Management System, as well as Halal Certified by the Islamic Foundation of Bangladesh. Today, IGFL continues to do best to create a healthy and affluent society in communication with our customers through our unique & quality products.
